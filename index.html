What I've Been Listening To

I'm back and I'm bad baby! :p



Metdog - One for the Kids (2025)

My favorite new album of the year so far!









Elvis II - Thank You Very Much (2025)

This rocks so fucking hard!









The Tubs - Cotton Crown (2025)

Their EP in 2021 was fantastic and their first LP was pretty good, but this second one is great!







Chronophage - Musical Attack: Communist + Anarchist Friendship (2025)

Great EP from a really cool Austin band! Very Austiny sounding to me, glad there's still some good music here!









R.M.F.C. - Ecstatic Strife (2025)

Cool new 2 song single from this super cool Australian band!









Munchkin Head/The Soda Jerk Split (2023)

More great weird music from Munchkin head, has a Judas Priest 'cover', side 2 has modern stand up comedy by a guy from Soda Boys!







Sandy Denny - Early Home Recordings (2022))

What a gift, Sandy Denny's early home recordings were all released on bandcamp! Folk music recorded at home probably when she was a teenager!







Hole Dweller - Flies in the Coop (2019)

I love this listening to dungeon synth when playing Hogwarts Legacy! One of my favorite dungeon core albums







Wesley Willis - Greatest Hits Vol 2 (2017)

"Get your ass to the barber shop and tell the barber you're sick of looking like an asshole"
