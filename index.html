What I've Been Listening To

Check out these choice new slabs!



Liquids - Tennessee Rose EP (2025)

Let's get crazy!









Wax Static - Beach (2024)

Music from Ohio makes me think of California, surfing and skateboarding!







Prison Affair - Demo 1 (re-release 2025)

An old egg punk demo re-released and on vinyl!







Repo Fam - Venus Fly (2021)

Some cool new york rock with no hooks! That's what some dj said anyway (what's a hook?) but whatever it is I like it!







Munchin Head - Not Gonna Cry Dad (2022)

Been listening to this band a lot lately!







Gee Tee - ChromoZone (2019)

More great rock! Egg punk or Devo core started some years ago when some high schoolers made an album that I also included!







Slayer - Hell Awaits (1985)

California youth with problems making some cool punky metal, good for skating to!







Slander Tongue - Let is Snow (2024)

More new underground rock, this time from Berlin! Bands like Liquor Store, Loosey kickin it loud!







Dukkha - Dukkha (2025)

How does MRnR find this stuff? Sounds like what we used to play in the garage, I love it!







Peru Ubu - Dub Housing (1978)

Very early post-punk to rock to!







Nosferatu - Society's Bastard tape (2022)

Great fast hardcore from Austin! There's still some music in this town!







Life Stinks - Life Stinks (2013)

Legendary band! I have both their LP's. The other one I don't think you can listen to it online without paying on iTunes or buying the record. It's really good too though, this band is great!
